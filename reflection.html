My Reflection

In everything I do, I believe “Una ang Ginoo” — God first. This webpage represents how faith guides my decisions, my actions, and the kind of digital citizen I aim to be.

As students living in the digital age, we are surrounded by information, technology, and online interactions. My goal is to use technology responsibly—to inspire, help, and uplift others.

The theme “Light of the World” reminds me that every post, message, and digital creation reflects who I am. I want to be that light—someone who spreads positivity, kindness, honesty, and respect online.

Through this project, I hope to show that faith and technology can work together to bring good things to the world.
