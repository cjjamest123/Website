Faith and Technology Working Together

Technology is a powerful tool, and when guided by faith, it becomes a way to spread inspiration, hope, and positive change. As students, we are called to use digital tools with purpose — to become a Light of the World, even online.

How Technology Helps Us Share God's Light

  • Sharing inspirational messages and Bible verses online
  • Creating digital art, websites, or videos that inspire others
  • Joining online faith communities and prayer groups
  • Spreading kindness and positivity on social media
  • Using technology for learning, ministry, and service

Faith-Based Digital Practices

Think before you post — reflect God’s love.

Use technology ethically and responsibly.

Share content that uplifts, not destroys.

Balance screen time with prayer and real-life connection.

A Message for Students

Technology is a gift. How we use it reflects who we are. When we bring our faith into the digital world, our actions online become part of our mission: to inspire, encourage, and shine light wherever we go.
