Helping Others Through Service

As followers of the principle “Light of the World,” we are encouraged to share kindness, compassion, and hope within our community. Community service is one of the greatest ways we can show God’s love to others—through action, compassion, and generosity.

In Tangub City and across the Philippines, many communities rely on students, youth groups, churches, and organizations to lend a helping hand. Whether it is participating in clean-up drives, feeding programs, charity events, or simply volunteering time, every act of service becomes a light that shines in the world.

Examples of Community Initiatives

✔ Barangay clean-up drives

✔ Church outreach and feeding programs

✔ Environmental awareness campaigns

✔ School charity missions

✔ Youth leadership and volunteer groups

These efforts remind us that serving others is not only good for the community, but also strengthens our character, faith, and sense of responsibility.

Why Community Service Matters

Community service teaches humility, empathy, and leadership. It helps us develop values that reflect our faith: kindness, patience, honesty, and love. By choosing to serve, we become instruments of hope and inspiration.
