Digital Ethics & Responsible Use of Technology

In a world where technology is everywhere, it is important to use it in a way that reflects the values of “Una ang Ginoo” (God First) and Light of the World. Good digital behavior shows respect, honesty, and compassion for others.

Core Values for Responsible Online Behavior

  • Be honest — avoid plagiarism and misinformation.
  • Be respectful — treat people online with courtesy and kindness.
  • Be responsible — think before you post.
  • Protect your privacy — use strong passwords and avoid oversharing.
  • Use technology to help, not harm.

Practical Tips for Students

Verify information before sharing.

Cite your sources properly.

Avoid cyberbullying and negativity.

Use technology to inspire and educate others.
